tri-ad lock


Cold Steel is perhaps best known for its invention of the Tri-Ad® lock, which they’ve proven through rigorous torture tests to be the most secure and safest folding knife locking system on the market. Designed by famed knife designer and Cold Steel collaborator Andrew Demko, the Tri-Ad®lock uses a unique combination of features that responds exceptionally to both positive and negative pressures and has been tested to hold up to 800 pounds of hanging weights.


atlas lock


Cold Steel's all new Atlas® Lock was engineered to blur the lines between folding and fixed blades. The patent pending Atlas® lock is incredibly durable, reliable, and quick to actuate. The stop pin buffers against and redistributes the force of the blade into the handle and away from the lock bar. The blade is shouldered around the stop pin for increased resistance to wear and tear. The stop pin buffers against, and redistributes the force pressure from the blade.


The lock bar is compressed between the blade tang and two pins. The force is evenly shared by the two pins creating the strongest lock on the market. The lock bar is positioned along the spine of the handle making it ambidextrous. The textured release switch sits within a notch in the handle scales keeping it out of the way until you need to close the blade.
